About Us

Our Services

Bathing and Drying

Give your pet the ultimate spa-like experience! Our bathing and drying service leaves them clean, fresh, and feeling their best. Regular baths not only keep coats shiny and healthy, but also remove dirt, odors, and loose hair—keeping your furry friend happier and healthier

  • Gentle bath with pet-safe products
  • Conditioning shampoo
  • Blow dry & coat fluffing

Haircuts and Styling

Turn heads on every walk! From breed-specific cuts to stylish trims, our professional groomers tailor each haircut to suit your pet’s personality and comfort. A neat, stylish coat isn’t just about looks—it helps prevent matting and keeps your pet cool and comfortable.

  • Breed-standard or custom haircut
  • Trimming and shaping
  • Styling to suit your pet’s look

Nail Trimming

Say goodbye to scratches and discomfort! Regular nail trimming keeps your pet’s paws healthy and prevents overgrown nails from causing pain or posture issues. Our gentle approach makes the process stress-free and safe for your furry friend.

  • Careful nail trimming
  • Paw pad check-up
  • Optional nail smoothing

Ear Cleaning

Healthy ears mean a happier pet! Our ear cleaning service removes dirt, wax, and debris to reduce the risk of infections and irritation. Gentle and thorough, it keeps your pet comfortable and smelling fresh.

  • Gentle ear cleaning
  • Wax & debris removal
  • Hygiene check for early signs of infection

Flea and Tick Treatments

Protect your pet from unwanted pests! Our flea and tick treatments are safe, effective, and designed to keep your pet itch-free and healthy. Regular treatments safeguard not just your pet, but also your home from infestations.

  • Flea & tick removal
  • Preventive treatment application
  • Coat and skin health check
